Welcome to Acumen Pvt. I.T.I.
Khari Kurd Shyampur, Rishikesh Dist Dheradun Uttrakhand

The Institute is a Acumen ITI set up under the aegis of 6/33/13/2016-TC in 2016 and Afflicted by D.G.E & T, National Council of Vocational Training, Ministry of Labor, Govt. of India, for providing quality technical education.

Education with:
  • Vision
  • Mission
  • Teaching Staff from recognized corporate
  • Technical training
  • On job training
  • Corporate placement

This Industrial Training Institute was established with the aim to help those youth who cannot go for higher education for one or the other reason and are interested to go for technical training. Initially, the Institute started imparting training in the following Trades on the pattern of I.T.I Courses and is waiting for the permanent affiliation of National Council for Vocational Training.
